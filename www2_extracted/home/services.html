Our Services

GOING TO THE AIRPORT

If you're heading to the airport, we provide transportation to and from Flesland Airport (BGO) in Bergen. Give us a call or send us an email for more information and fixed pricing.

WHEELCHAIR

Voss Taxi has a diverse fleet of vehicles with space for foldable wheelchairs, but if you need to bring an electric wheelchair or need to remain in the wheelchair during transport, we have two 16-seat MaxiTaxi vans with space for 2 users simultaneously. Additionally, we have 3 units of 8-seat wheelchair-accessible vans, all equipped with ramps for easy boarding. See the dedicated tab for more information and contact details.

LOST PROPERTY

Have you lost something? Please contact us.

We regularly receive items such as mobile phones, gloves, hats, bags, glasses, scarves, and umbrellas.

All items found in our vehicles are kept at our central office located in the heart of Voss town center.

If you are missing something, please give us a call, send an email, or drop by.

Clothing items are usually returned to their rightful owners if they are marked with a name and number. In such cases, we typically reach out to the owner. Other forgotten textiles are usually disposed of, or donated to charity.

Valuables such as money, mobile phones, cameras, and tablets, will be handed over to the police station in Voss, if the owner does not show up within a reasonable time.

BIKES?

We also have the possibility to bring bikes. Please inform us in advance, and we'll bring a rack or a trailer.

JUMP STARTS

If there's a crisis and you experience a flat battery, we also have a power booster available if needed.

GUIDE DOGS

All our vehicles and drivers accommodate guide dogs.

CHILDREN IN THE CAR

We have child seats with IsoFix and support base for children of all ages. In addition, we have a wide range of booster seats and booster cushions. You can also borrow seats if you need them for a short trip or two.

We have vehicles for all types of tasks!

Maxi-Taxi & Wheelchair

Minibuses with room for 16 passengers or 2 wheelchairs at the same time

MiniVan

Minivans with room for 7-8 passengers

Sedans

Sedans with room for 1-4 passengers
